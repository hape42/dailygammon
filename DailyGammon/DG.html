Welcome to DailyGammon app

A way to play backgammon games and tournaments with real people from around the world, all at once. It is free and ad-free.

Play is at your own pace with opponents who usually won’t be online at the same time as you. You can have dozens of games going all at once.

This DailyGammon app is a client for the DailyGammon.com web service, started over 20 years ago. You can play directly from a web browser like Safari, but from iPhone or iPad, the web-play experience is challenging.

This app helps by improving display, moves, and chat. And it can notify when you have games ready to play.

Getting started is a bit Complicated. Here’s the process:

  • Register and confirm DailyGammon login.
  • Login through this app.
  • Visit Game Lounge (menu button top-right).
  • Find "Beginners Tournament" and Sign Up.
  • Wait for tournament to start, after enough others have signed up (a few hours to days).
  • Visit Top Page to see games where you can play, and tap opponent name to play.

The hardest part to starting is getting your DailyGammon account setup.

Be sure to enter a correct email and remember your chosen username. You should get email with your initial password within 24 hours. If you don’t receive it, email dghelp@gmail.com and include your requested username.

We hope that’s enough to get you started. There are more new-user hints at http://www.dailygammon.com/help/hints.html.

Note that this app is NOT built by the creators of the DailyGammon service.
